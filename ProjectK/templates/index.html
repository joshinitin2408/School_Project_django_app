Welcome to Global Innovative School Parasia

GLOBAL INNOVATIVE SCHOOL PARASIA is a Co-educational, exclusively English medium school. Our institution is private unaided, established, managed and run by Vidya Niketan Education and Welfare Society. The school is committed to give identical opportunities to boys and girls. Keeping in view that “Child is the most vital raw material in the world to be worked on”. The school offers quality education coupled with extra curricular activities aiming at sanskaras based on one’s culture, personality traits and bodily fitness which is also the need of the hour

Location

GLOBAL INNOVATIVE SCHOOL PARASIA is located 4Km. from the heart of Parasia town on Tamia-Bypass Road near Rajeev Gandhi Stadium (C.I.S.F Camp).The founding members were conscious about the selection of the spacious land with beautiful surroundings,peacefull atmosphere which is soothing to the eyes in the midst of nature.
